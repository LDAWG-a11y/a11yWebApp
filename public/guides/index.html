  • Accessibility Statements - what are they?

    An accessibility statement is a specific legal document that organisations must publish under the Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations (PSBAR). The statement must include information about accessibility issues on a system, contact information, enforcement procedure, as well as several other legally required sections. This guide explains what they are, who needs one, what the scope is, and how they are checked.

Need help?

If you require assistance with anything on our site at all, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us, we aim to promptly reply to all requests. If you have trouble with any part of our site, please let us know and one of our experienced team will identify and resolve the problem, this of course includes if any part of the site does not work as intended with you assistive technology, if possible, please provide details of browser, assistive technology and a URL to the issue.

Please feel free to email us at: info@makethingsaccessible.com, and we will respond as soon as we can.
