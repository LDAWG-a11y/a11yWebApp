  • What are the Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations?

    The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No.2) Accessibility Regulations 2018 are a set of laws requiring public sector bodies to proactively make their digital estates accessible for both internal and external facing content. The regulations do this by requiring compliance with technical standards through the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1.
