Need help?

If you have trouble with accessing any part of our site or links, please let us know and one of our experienced team will identify and resolve the problem. If any part of the site does not work as intended with your assistive technology, please provide details of the browser, assistive technology you're using, and the URL wherever possible.

Please feel free to email us at: info@makethingsaccessible.com, and we will respond as soon as we can.

Theme preference
Themes help
  • Light: will typically be darker text on lighter backgrounds (with limited exceptions), all contrast exceeds all WCAG 2.2 requirements at least to Level AA
  • Reset: is the default setting, should you have chosen a theme in on your device settings, that choice will be displayed. This option also serves to remove the preference token from your browser's local storage
  • Dark: this option is typically lighter text on darker backgrounds and also surpasses WCAG 2.2 requirements
Motion preference
Motion help
  • Subtle: will have subtle animations, such as transitioning from one colour to another to indicate an item has focus or is being hovered, components that expand will have an expanding collapsing animation and smooth scrolling is set
  • Reset obeys your operating reset preferences, fade-in and fade-out animations are still present, but no scaling or expanding & collapsing animations are present. Smooth scrolling is disabled. this setting also removes the token in your browser's local storage
  • Off, completely removes all animations, including fade-in and fade-out and smooth scrolling is also disabled
